## Opinions

I've come to love **Swift** for building apps — in my experience, it strikes
a balance between expressiveness and safety that makes the development process
genuinely enjoyable. I've grown equally fond of **Rust** as a systems language;
what I appreciate most is how it nudges you toward writing correct code by
design, which has shaped the way I think about reliability. And Apple's
developer ecosystem — Xcode, Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit — is, in my view, the most
architecturally coherent platform I've worked with. The consistency it offers
across iOS, macOS, watchOS, and visionOS is something I've come to deeply
appreciate.
