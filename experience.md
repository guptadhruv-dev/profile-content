## RootKit's Inception
RootKit began as a student club at MIT World Peace University while I was an undergraduate in Computational Mathematics and Statistics. I taught myself iOS development, then built and published many iOS apps. The club pulled in students from engineering and design and grew into a working freelance operation.

The turning point arrived when the **Pro-Vice-Chancellor** & the **IT Director** of MIT-WPU handed us the responsibility to manage the IT infrastructure for the **National Legislature Conference Bharat 2023**. We built MIT-WPU Connect, the flagship campus application, which earned recognition from the founder and president of [MIT Group of Institutions](https://www.mitindia.com/) - **Dr. Vishwanath Karad** and **Mr. Rahul Karad**. With that momentum and a strong core team, we formally incorporated the consultancy with three partners in April 2023, and many of our earliest clients came along for the larger work.
